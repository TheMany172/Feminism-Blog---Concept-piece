Radical Feminism: Theory and Practice

March 17, 2024 Feminist Theory

Radical feminism emerged as a powerful force in feminist theory, challenging traditional power structures and advocating for fundamental social change. This article examines its principles and contemporary relevance.

Core Principles

Key tenets include:

  • Patriarchy as primary oppression
  • Personal is political
  • Consciousness-raising
  • Systemic change
  • Women's liberation

Historical Context

Important developments:

  • Second wave feminism
  • Women's liberation movement
  • Feminist consciousness-raising groups
  • Radical feminist organizations
  • Influence on feminist theory

Contemporary Applications

Modern relevance includes:
